## Quick Answer

**Quick Answer:** Enter income and expense lines (`name amount` per row) in our [Budget Calculator](/tools/finance/budget-calculator) to see **total income**, **expenses**, **balance**, and **savings rate**.

## Why this matters

A quick monthly snapshot helps you spot overspending before it becomes debt.

## Step by step

1. Open the [Budget Calculator](/tools/finance/budget-calculator).
2. Add a base income figure plus optional income lines.
3. List expenses as `Rent 1200` style rows and read the summary card.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### What line format works?

One item per line: label then amount, e.g. `Groceries 400`.

### Is this financial advice?

No—planning helper only. Consult professionals for tax or investment decisions.

### Is data stored?

No. Calculations run **locally in your browser**.
