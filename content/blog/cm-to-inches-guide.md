## Quick Answer

**Quick Answer:** 1 inch = **2.54 cm**. To convert cm to inches, divide by 2.54. **170 cm = 66.93 inches (5 ft 7 in)**. Use our [Length Converter](/tools/convert/length).

## Common conversions

| cm | inches | feet (approx) |
|----|--------|---------------|
| 160 | 62.99 | 5'3" |
| 170 | 66.93 | 5'7" |
| 180 | 70.87 | 5'11" |

Divide cm by 2.5 for a rough inch estimate (slightly low).
