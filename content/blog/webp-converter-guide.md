## Quick Answer

## Why this matters

Modern sites use WebP for smaller files; sometimes you still need PNG/JPEG for email or legacy systems.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Which formats are supported?

WebP, PNG, and JPEG output from common input images.

### Is quality adjustable?

Yes—for WebP and JPEG. PNG is lossless (no quality slider).

### Are images uploaded?

No. Conversion uses canvas **locally in your browser**.
