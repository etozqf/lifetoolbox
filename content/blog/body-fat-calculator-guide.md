## Quick Answer

**Quick Answer:** Enter height, neck, waist (and hip for women) in centimeters in our [Body Fat Calculator](/tools/health/body-fat-calculator) for a **US Navy method** body fat estimate.

## Why this matters

BMI alone does not distinguish muscle from fat; circumference formulas add another data point for fitness tracking.

## Step by step

1. Open the [Body Fat Calculator](/tools/health/body-fat-calculator).
2. Select sex and enter metric measurements.
3. Read the estimated percentage and medical disclaimer.

## Tips

- Compare with [BMI Calculator](/tools/health/bmi-calculator) and [Ideal Weight](/tools/health/ideal-weight).
- Measure consistently—same time of day, relaxed stomach.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is this a medical test?

No. It is an **estimate** for education only—not a diagnosis.

### Why is hip required for women?

The Navy formula uses hip circumference in the female equation.

### Are measurements uploaded?

No. Math runs **locally in your browser**.
