## Frequently Asked Questions

### What lengths are required?

EAN-13: 12 or 13 digits. UPC: 11 or 12 digits. CODE128: up to 80 characters.

### Can I print these commercially?

Verify symbology specs and quiet zones for your printer—this tool is for quick drafts.

### Is generation local?

Yes—JsBarcode runs **in your browser**.
