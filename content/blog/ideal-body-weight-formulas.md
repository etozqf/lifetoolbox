## Quick Answer

**Quick Answer:** Ideal body weight formulas estimate a healthy weight for your height. For a 5'7" (170 cm) woman, the Devine formula gives about **61 kg (134 lb)**. Use our [Ideal Weight Calculator](/tools/health/ideal-weight).

## Common formulas

| Formula | Best for |
|---------|----------|
| Devine (1974) | General clinical estimate |
| Robinson (1983) | Slightly lower estimates |
| Miller (1983) | Alternative clinical reference |

## Limitations

These formulas do not account for muscle mass, frame size, or ethnicity. They are screening tools, not diagnoses.
