## Quick Answer

**Quick Answer:** A common guideline is **33 ml per kg of body weight** per day. A 70 kg (154 lb) person should drink about **2.3 liters (77 oz)** daily. Use our [Water Intake Calculator](/tools/health/water-intake).

## The 8-glasses rule

"8 glasses a day" (~2 liters) is a rough average, not personalized. Weight-based formulas are more tailored.

## Imperial rule of thumb

Some guidelines suggest **half your body weight in ounces** of water per day (e.g., 160 lb → 80 oz).

## Factors that increase needs

Exercise, hot weather, pregnancy, and illness increase fluid requirements.
