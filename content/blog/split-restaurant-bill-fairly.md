## Quick Answer

**Quick Answer:** The fairest way to split a bill is **evenly** when everyone ordered similarly, or **by item** when orders differ widely. For a $120 bill among 4 people with 18% tip, each person pays **$35.40**. Use our [Bill Splitter](/tools/calc/bill-splitter).

## Even split vs itemized split

**Even split** works best when the group ordered similar amounts. **Itemized split** is fairer when one person had steak and others had salads.

## Step-by-step even split

1. Add the bill total (including tax if you tip on after-tax amount).
2. Add tip (typically 15–20%).
3. Divide by the number of people.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do you split a bill with tip?

Calculate total with tip first, then divide by the number of people.

### Should tax be included before splitting?

Most groups split the final amount everyone pays at the table, including tax and tip.
