## Quick Answer

**Quick Answer:** Sale price = Original price × (1 − discount ÷ 100). A **25% off** deal on a **$80** item costs **$60**. Try our [Discount Calculator](/tools/calc/discount-calculator).

## The discount formula

If an item is $49.99 and the discount is 20%:
- Savings = $49.99 × 0.20 = **$10.00**
- Sale price = **$39.99**

## Black Friday mental math

For 30% off, multiply the price by 0.70. For 50% off, halve the price.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do I find 15% off a price?

Multiply the price by 0.15 for savings, or use our discount tool.

### Is the sale price before or after tax?

Stores usually apply discounts before sales tax; tax is calculated on the discounted price.
