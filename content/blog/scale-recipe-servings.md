## Quick Answer

Quick Answer: Multiply every ingredient by **(new servings ÷ original servings)**. Doubling a 4-serving recipe to 8? Multiply each amount by **2**. Try the [Recipe Scaler](/tools/kitchen/recipe-scaler).
