## Quick Answer

**Quick Answer:** Daily calorie needs depend on age, sex, weight, height, and activity. A moderately active 30-year-old woman at 170 cm and 70 kg needs about **2,000–2,200 kcal/day**. Calculate yours with our [Calorie Calculator](/tools/health/calorie-calculator).

## Mifflin-St Jeor equation

The Mifflin-St Jeor BMR formula is widely used:
- **Men:** BMR = 10×weight(kg) + 6.25×height(cm) − 5×age + 5
- **Women:** BMR = 10×weight(kg) + 6.25×height(cm) − 5×age − 161

Multiply BMR by an activity factor (1.2–1.9) for total daily energy expenditure.

## Activity multipliers

| Level | Factor |
|-------|--------|
| Sedentary | ×1.2 |
| Light exercise | ×1.375 |
| Moderate | ×1.55 |
| Active | ×1.725 |
